Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas, smashed
  • 2 cups flour
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

Notes on Ingredients

Sugar
You can increase or decrease the quantity of sugar based on individual tastes.
Eggs
You can replace eggs with milk if you want.

Procedure

  1. Preheat the oven to 175Â°C.
  2. Mix butter into the mashed bananas in a large mixing bowl with a spoon.
  3. Mix in the egg, sugar and vanilla.
  4. Sprinkle the baking soda over the mixture and mix in
  5. Add the flour and mix.
  6. Pour mixture into a buttered loaf pan.
  7. Bake for 45 minutes. Remove from pan once cooled and slice to serve.
